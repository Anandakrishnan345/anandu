Delicious Chocolate Chip Cookies

prepration time : 30 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vannila extract
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. preheat oven to 350F
  2. in a large bowl,cream together the butter and the sugar until smooths
  3. beat the eggs one at a time then stir in the vannila
  4. in a seperate bowl, combine flour and baking soda gragually add it to butter mixture
  5. stir in the chocolate chips
  6. drop by rounded tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheets
  7. bake for 10-12mins or until edges are golden brown
